About Sooqia

Founded in Riyadh, Sooqia is an up-and-coming e-commerce brand that curates high-quality products for your home, lifestyle and wellbeing. Our mission is to make smart shopping simple and joyful by focusing on quality, value and service. We believe that shopping should be convenient, trustworthy and rewarding. While our own online storefront is under construction, we invite you to explore our collection on trusted marketplaces.

Mission

To enable customers to shop smart and live better through a curated selection of everyday essentials and innovative products, delivered with exceptional care.

Vision

To become a trusted regional brand that redefines online shopping by putting people, quality and sustainability first.

Values

Customer-centricity, honesty, quality, innovation and respect for our community and environment.
